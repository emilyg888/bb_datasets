# Business Glossary

This glossary defines business terms used in the synthetic Element Fleet Services Australia / Custom Fleet dataset.

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| ABN | Australian Business Number. The dataset uses synthetic ABN-style identifiers only. |
| Accident management | Managed process for driver incident support, claim lodgement, repair allocation, replacement vehicle coordination, recoveries, and claim reporting. |
| Asset type | Vehicle category such as passenger vehicle, SUV, ute, van, light commercial vehicle, or truck. |
| Auction channel | Disposal pathway used to sell a returned or end-of-lease vehicle, such as wholesale auction, online auction, tender, or dealer direct. |
| BEV | Battery electric vehicle. A vehicle powered only by an electric battery and motor. |
| Billing period | Month or period covered by an invoice. |
| CTP | Compulsory Third Party insurance, a state or territory personal injury insurance requirement linked to Australian vehicle registration. |
| Charger ID | Synthetic identifier for an EV charger asset used in a charging session. |
| Client | Organization receiving fleet leasing or fleet-management services. |
| Client portal | Digital self-service or reporting channel used by fleet clients to raise requests, view updates, download reports, or track cases. |
| Condition grade | Synthetic remarketing assessment of a vehicle's condition before disposal. |
| Driver app | Mobile app used by drivers for tasks such as odometer updates, service bookings, damage reports, fuel support, registration reminders, or EV charge receipts. |
| Email domain | Synthetic organization domain used for contact, billing, claims, or portal routing fields. |
| Effective at | Source timestamp showing when a record version became business-effective. Used for mutable records that can change over time. |
| EV | Electric vehicle. In this dataset, EV includes BEV and PHEV vehicles where charging sessions may apply. |
| EV charging session | Charging event for an electric or plug-in hybrid vehicle, including energy consumed, provider, location type, and reimbursement status. |
| Finance lease | Lease structure where financing is central and the lessee may carry more residual or ownership-like economics than under an operating lease. |
| Fleet management only | Service arrangement where the provider manages fleet operations without necessarily financing the vehicle lease. |
| Fuel card | Vehicle-linked payment card or account used for fuel or related fleet purchases. |
| Fuel exception | Transaction that may require review, such as unusual location, amount, product type, or usage pattern. |
| Harsh event | Telematics-detected driving event such as harsh braking, acceleration, or cornering. |
| Included services | Services bundled into a lease or management contract, such as maintenance, fuel, registration, accident management, telematics, billing, or driver app support. |
| In service date | Date a vehicle starts active use in the fleet. |
| Lease end date | Expected end date for the lease or fleet assignment. |
| Lease product | Commercial product type, such as operating lease, finance lease, novated lease, or fleet management only. |
| Maintenance vendor | Supplier that performs scheduled servicing, tyres, glass, batteries, mechanical repairs, or mobile maintenance. |
| Management fee | Recurring fee charged for administering fleet services. |
| Novated lease | Employee vehicle lease arrangement commonly administered through an employer or salary packaging structure. |
| Odometer | Distance reading for a vehicle, measured in kilometres. |
| Operating lease | Lease product where the fleet provider typically retains residual value exposure and the client pays for vehicle use over a term. |
| PHEV | Plug-in hybrid electric vehicle with both a battery/motor and internal combustion engine. |
| Preferred vendor | Supplier included in a preferred or approved service network. |
| Registration renewal | Administrative process for keeping a vehicle legally registered in a state or territory. |
| Reimbursement status | State of payment matching or reimbursement for an EV charging session or driver-paid cost. |
| Remarketing | Process of disposing of returned or end-of-lease vehicles through auction, tender, dealer, or wholesale channels. |
| Residual value | Estimated vehicle value at the end of the lease term. |
| Sale amount | Final amount achieved through disposal or auction. |
| Service category | Type of maintenance or repair, such as scheduled service, tyres, brakes, windscreen, battery, or mechanical repair. |
| Telematics | Vehicle data collected through GPS, OEM connected services, or installed devices, often used for utilization, safety, route, and performance analysis. |
| Telematics device | Synthetic hardware or connected-vehicle device assigned to a vehicle and referenced by telematics summaries. |
| Term months | Contract duration in months. |
| Updated at | Source timestamp showing when a mutable source-system record was last updated. Useful for incremental loads, change detection, and audit trails. |
| Uptime | Measure of vehicle availability for productive use. Downtime can be caused by maintenance, repair, claims, or registration issues. |
| Vehicle lifecycle | End-to-end flow from acquisition and lease setup through in-life management, billing, risk support, EV operations, and end-of-lease disposal. |
| Vehicle status | Current operational state of a vehicle, such as in service, off road, pending disposal, or closed. |
| VIN | Vehicle Identification Number. The dataset uses synthetic 17-character VIN-style values only. |
| Work order | Maintenance or repair job raised for a vehicle and assigned to a vendor. |
